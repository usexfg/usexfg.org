# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Sovereign individuals (Privacy Seekers) and Traders (Yield Farmers) looking for private sound money and decentralized financial tools. They value privacy, hard-capped supply, and technical excellence.

## Product Purpose
To establish Fuego (XFG) as the premier private blockchain bank. The site introduces the ecosystem, including the HEAT stablecoin, Hearth AMM, Certificates of Deposit, and trustless atomic swaps, driving adoption and wallet downloads.

## Brand Personality
Sovereign, Magnificent, Innovative, Luxury. A high-end technical aesthetic that feels established, precise, and boundary-pushing.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS cliches (bright, rounded, over-saturated), "Childish" Crypto/Cyberpunk cliches (sci-fi fonts like Chakra Petch or Audiowide, neon glows), and decorative "fluff" or layers (excessive glassmorphism).

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Confidence**: Every pixel should reflect top-tier engineering and financial precision.
2. **Sovereign Privacy**: Evoke a sense of seclusion, security, and total individual control.
3. **Magnificent Minimalism**: Achieve luxury through intentional spacing and superior typography, not decorative elements.
4. **Technical Clarity**: Demystify complex financial flywheels (like HEAT and YIELD) through structural hierarchy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast for technical data (DM Mono) and ensure Orbitron headings remain legible across devices. Standard WCAG alignment for a dark-themed, high-fidelity experience.
